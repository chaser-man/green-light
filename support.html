Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the text analysis?

Green Light uses advanced AI to analyze text conversations. While it's designed to identify common relationship communication patterns, it's not perfect and should be used as a helpful tool rather than the final word. Always trust your instincts and use Green Light as a supplement to your own judgment.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority. Screenshots are processed locally on your device, and we don't store your conversations. Our AI analysis happens securely and any data transmitted is encrypted. You can use Green Light with confidence knowing your personal conversations remain private.

Can I analyze conversations from any messaging app?

Yes! Green Light works with screenshots from any messaging platform - iMessage, WhatsApp, Instagram, Tinder, Bumble, or any other app where you have conversations. Just take a screenshot and upload it to Green Light for analysis.

How often is the AI updated?

We regularly update our AI model to improve accuracy and keep up with evolving communication trends. Updates are automatic when you update the app, so you'll always have the latest analysis capabilities without any extra steps.

Can I delete my data from Green Light?

Green Light doesn't store your conversations on our servers. All processing happens on your device or through secure, temporary API calls. When you delete the app or clear your app data, all information is completely removed.
